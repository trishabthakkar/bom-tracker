Engineering Change Order: ECO-2026-0712
Title: Replace pressure relief valve in cooling manifold assembly

Change type: part replacement
Old part: PN-1212
New part: PN-2212
Reason: The current pressure relief valve PN-1212 is being replaced because the supplier has issued an end-of-life notice and the replacement valve has improved pressure stability for field service conditions.
Effective date: 2026-08-15

Affected product context:
The change applies to the Cooling Manifold Assembly used in the Cooling Skid Final Assembly.

Implementation notes:
- Replace PN-1212 Pressure relief valve with PN-2212 High-stability pressure relief valve.
- Review procurement records for supplier, lead time, cost, and approved vendor details.
- Review installation guide torque and orientation instructions for the valve.
- Review commissioning pressure test procedure and acceptance criteria.
- Review service manual replacement procedure and spare parts list.

Expected risk:
Medium. The affected part is inside the cooling manifold path and may impact procurement, installation, commissioning, and service documentation.
